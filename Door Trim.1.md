
# Door Trim

[TOC]

## Removing
1. Score the joints/caulk with a utility knife
2. Pry off with putty knife/chisel
3. Remove old caulk with a putty knife/chisel
## Installing
1. Install with 1 1/4 brads
2. calk the seams
3. Cut at 30 deg angle
